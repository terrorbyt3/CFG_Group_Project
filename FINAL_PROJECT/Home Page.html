About

The Endangered Animals Collective is working towards a world where animals and people live in harmony. We aim to secure and enhance animal populations for future generations to enjoy. We are committed to reversing the trend of human-induced species extinction, saving endangered animals, and preserving wild places for these animals to thrive. We run several initiatives globally to help with caring for and encouraging these animals in the wild and your local areas.

Meaning of Endangered

Endangered is a word used to describe animal species that are not doing very well and are at risk of dying out. An animal that is endangered is close to extinction, meaning there aren't very many of that type of animal left in the world either in their natural habitat in the wild or in captivity. The biggest threat to animals comes from humans. Animals usually become endangered because of over hunting or the destruction of their natural habitat.

Conservation

The protection of endangered animals is called conservation. As humans have become more aware of the dangers affecting wild animals they have started to take steps to stop animals from becoming extinct. Examples of this range from the introduction of international laws prohibiting hunting and selling of rare animal products, protected national parks and wildlife reserves, to breeding programmes which increase numbers in in captivity to eventually reintroduce to wild habitats.
